EHS Environment, Health & Safety
Regulatory framework ensuring workplace safety and environmental compliance. In pharma, EHS governs hazardous material handling, labelling, and classification.
GHS Globally Harmonized System
International standard for classifying and labelling hazardous chemicals (e.g., H302 = "Harmful if swallowed"). Required on all API raw materials before manufacturing.
MDG Master Data Governance
SAP module for managing master data (materials, vendors, customers). All material changes go through MDG staging → workflow approval → production commit.
GxP Good Practice (GMP/GLP/GCP)
Regulatory quality guidelines for pharma manufacturing (GMP), labs (GLP), and clinical trials (GCP). Systems touching production data require validated audit trails.
PII/PHI Personal / Health Information
PII = personally identifiable data (name, DOB). PHI = protected health information (medical records). Both governed by GDPR with mandatory masking requirements.
SCC Standard Contractual Clauses
EU-approved legal mechanism for transferring personal data outside the EEA. Required for any EU → US data movement under GDPR.
DQ Data Quality
Measured across dimensions: completeness, accuracy, format validity, uniqueness, timeliness. AZ threshold is 95% for production-ready data.
UoM Unit of Measure
Measurement unit for materials (KG, L, EA). Supplier APIs often use different standards (ISO 20022) than SAP (internal codes), causing mapping errors.
CAS Chemical Abstracts Service
Unique numerical identifier for chemical substances (e.g., 103-90-2 = Acetaminophen). Used to look up GHS hazard classifications from reference databases.
CR Change Request
SAP MDG workflow object. Every data change creates a CR (e.g., CR-28491) that must be approved through the MDG governance workflow before production commit.
